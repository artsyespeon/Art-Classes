Courses

We have six different courses, pottery, sketching, drawing/coloring, painting, crafting, and all. We do 2 classes everyday, one for 5-10 year olds and another for 11-14 year olds. On monday we do pottery, tuesday is sketching, wednesday and saturday are all, thursday is drawing/coloring, friday is painting, and sunday is crafting.
